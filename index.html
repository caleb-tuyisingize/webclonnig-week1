What is FeWo-direkt?


FeWo-direkt is your competent contact for holidays in a holiday home or apartment. As part of Vrbo, one of the leading online marketplaces for vacation rental rentals, FeWo-direkt offers you more than two million unique accommodations, including fincas, bungalows, apartments, holiday cabins and even villas. Secure payments, round-the-clock support and the Book with Confidence Guarantee make bookings a breeze. Via the user-friendly website and numerous practical filters, you can easily find your dream holiday apartment for your budget. So you are sure to spend an unforgettable holiday, which is stress-free from the search to the booking to the stay and departure.


Renting a holiday apartment or holiday home: These are the advantages


Families and groups in particular appreciate the advantages of holiday accommodation: more space, more privacy, more amenities. You have more flexibility and freedom in your own four walls than in conventional hotels and can plan your holiday freely - regardless of whether you want to go on a city trip or switch off with your dog in the middle of picturesque nature. Are you looking for a cozy holiday cabin in the mountains with a washing machine and hot tub? A beach house on the water or a large finca in the south with a pool and fully equipped kitchen? No problem, because holiday accommodations are available in all sizes and with every desired equipment you have in mind for your next family holiday, adventure trip with friends or a romantic weekend.
